Meter Type Monthly Cost

Summary

This page provides data about how much each type of meter costs during every month at TCNJ. We can see that on average the school spends the most per month on natural gas throughout the year, however the electric grid is not far behind. Some other interesting things to note include that electric is on average most expensive during September while Natural Gas is most expensive during November (note: November is the most expensive month overall) and both natural gas and electric are least expensive during October (note: October is the least expensive month overall).
